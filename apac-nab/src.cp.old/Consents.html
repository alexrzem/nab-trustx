Privacy Notification

In addition to the below, please also read our Privacy notification. These documents contain more detailed information about our collection, use, and handling of personal information. Our Privacy policy also containes our Credit Reporting Policy and information about how you can:

  • request access to or the correction of your information
  • make a privacy-related complaint
  • contact the credit reporting bodies we deal with if you have queries about the information they hold

We may exchange personal information about you with credit reporting bodies

We may obtain credit information about you from credit reporting bodies, on one or more occasion, to assess your application or to collect any payment that is overdue in relation to credit that we provide as a result of your application. If you may a further application for credit within 30 days of us obtaining your credit information from a credit reporting body, it may also be used in relation to that subsequent application.

If a NAB Credit account is provided to you as a result of this Application, we may disclose information about this to the credit reporting bodies, including:

  • the date the account is opened (and closed)
  • the account type and credit limit
  • your repayment history
  • any temporary or permanent hardship arrangements
  • any defaults or serious crime infringements

When we give your information to a credit reporting body, it may be included in reports that the credit reporting body gives other organisations (such as other lenders) to help them assess your credit worthiness. More information about your credit information handling practices and how to contact the credit reporting bodies is contained in our Credit Reporting Policy (please refer to Attachment 1 of our Privacy Policy), available on our website or on request).
